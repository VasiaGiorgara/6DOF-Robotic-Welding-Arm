# 6DOF-Robotic-Welding-Arm
# 6 DOF Robotic Welding Machine (Work in Progress)

### Project Overview
As the **Team Leader** of the Mediterranean College Robotics Team, I am leading the design and development of a 6 Degrees of Freedom (DOF) robotic arm specialized for industrial welding applications.

### Technical Specifications
* **Degrees of Freedom:** 6 Axis for full spatial orientation.
* **Target Application:** Precision arc welding.
* **Controller:** Arduino-based control system.
* **Focus Areas:** Inverse Kinematics, Motion Planning, and Industrial Automation.

### Current Status
* **Phase 1:** Structural Design & Simulation (Ongoing).
* **Phase 2:** Electronic Component Integration.
